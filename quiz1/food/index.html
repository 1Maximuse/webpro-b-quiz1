Anyone hungry?

I sure am. Here are some of the best in town.

Rawon Setan

Jalan Embong Malang No.78/I, Genteng, Surabaya

If you haven't tried rawon, you're missing out half of Surabaya's culinary.

Rawon Setan might be the most popular Rawon restaurant in Indonesia. Makes sense though, considering their spectacular rawon that melts in your mouth like no other. There's rawon, then there's Rawon Setan. Beware however, they make them extra spicy! Those who have weak bowels, brace yourselves!

Tahu Telor Pak Jayen

Jalan Dharmahusada Indah C-3 No.41, Mulyorejo, Surabaya

Opens only at dusk, this is the perfect place for saturday-night hangouts with your friends.

Tahu Telor, also frequently named Tahu Tek, is one of the most well known and most enjoyed local delicacies in town. This place makes one like no other, their recipe consisting of a mouthful of sweet mouth-watering peanut sauce complemented with a hint of garlic. Your taste buds will thank you.

Bakwan Pak Nur

Jalan Trunojoyo No.37, Dr. Soetomo, Tegalsari, Surabaya

"Place ain't much, but the food's dope, yo", younger me would say.

Located just near the bustling center of Surabaya is one small, yet always-crowded destination for those who love bakwan. This is Bakwan Pak Nur. While the place looks nothing out of the ordinary, it is regarded as one of the best bakwan places in town. Cheap prices and great taste will make every single one of the customers leaving full-bellied and happy.
