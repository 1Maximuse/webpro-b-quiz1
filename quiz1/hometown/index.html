I was born and raised in

Surabaya

A beautiful urban city located just under the strait of Madura.

Its name originates from the Javanese phrase "sura ing baya" meaning "bravely facing danger", much akin to the unrelenting bravery of freedom fighters during the Battle of Surabaya.

Bustling, yet inciting profound feelings. Gorgeous city, gorgeous people. This is Surabaya.
