Tourism in Surabaya

Suramadu National Bridge

Jalan Tol Suramadu, Tambak Wedi, Kenjeran, Surabaya

The longest bridge in the whole of Indonesia, surely worth a visit!

It connects two major Indonesian islands, the Java island and the Madura island. The best part, you don't even need to go out of town! Whether you're sightseeing from afar, or taking a ride through, you'll catch a glimpse of the breathtaking architectural marvel that is the Suramadu National Bridge.

Klenteng Sanggar Agung

Jalan Sukolilo No. 100, Surabaya

Facing dead east, this might just be the best spot for sunrise viewing in Surabaya.

This temple is located right on the shore under the Madura strait. Upon entering, your sight will be blessed with a beautiful and serene view of the sea horizon. Originally a place for the followers of Tridharma, it is now open as a tourism destination for the public.

Redback Specialty Coffee

Jalan Raya Golf Graha Famili No.2, Pradahkalikendal, Dukuhpakis, Surabaya

For the coffee lovers out there, there's no better place than Redback!

The place boasts a semi-outdoor style coffee shop, facing straight towards a calm and refreshing golf course. That along with the coffee makes it place a great place to wind down when you need to get work off your mind.
